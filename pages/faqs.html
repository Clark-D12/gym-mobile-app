FAQs

Frequently asked questions

Getting Started
🚀 How do I start a workout?
›

Go to the Training tab, pick a workout category (Chest, Abs, Legs, etc.), choose your level (Rookie, Veteran, Master), then tap Start. The app will guide you through each exercise with a timer.

👤 How do I change my username?
›

Go to Account → My Profile, type your new username in the field, then tap Save Changes.

🔑 How do I change my password?
›

Go to Account → My Profile, fill in the New Password and Confirm Password fields, then tap Save Changes. Passwords must be at least 6 characters.

Streaks & Progress
🔥 How does the streak work?
›

Your streak counts consecutive days you complete at least one workout. If you skip a day, your current streak resets to 1. Complete a workout every day to keep it going!

🏅 Do achievements go away if I miss a day?
›

No! Achievements are permanent. Day badges (D-3, D-7…) are based on your best streak ever, not your current streak. Exercise badges (E-3, E-7…) are based on total workouts completed, which never resets. Once earned, always earned. 🏆

📊 How is my BMI calculated?
›

BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height (m)². Go to the Reports tab and tap Edit BMI to enter your weight and height. The app will calculate and display your BMI category automatically.

📈 What does the progress chart show?
›

The bar chart on the Reports page shows how many workouts you completed each day this month, giving you a visual overview of your consistency over time.

Workouts
🏋️ What's the difference between Rookie, Veteran, and Master?
›

Rookie — 10 min, 5 exercises. Great for beginners.
Veteran — 15 min, 7 exercises. Moderate intensity.
Master — 20 min, 10 exercises. High intensity for advanced users.

⏸️ Can I pause a workout?
›

You can tap Prev to go back to the previous exercise at any time. To stop the session entirely, tap the ✕ close button — the workout won't be saved unless you complete all exercises.

💾 When does a workout get saved?
›

A workout is only saved when you complete all exercises and tap Finish ✓. Closing the session early does not count toward your stats or streak.

Account
🔔 How do I enable reminders?
›

Go to Account → Reminder and tap Yes, Enable. The app will remind you to work out each day so you keep your streak alive.

🚪 How do I log out?
›

Scroll to the bottom of the Account page and tap the Logout button. Confirm in the dialog and you'll be signed out safely.

🗑️ Can I delete my account?
›

To request account deletion, please use the Feedback page and select Other as the category. Our team will process your request within 7 business days.
